Creamy Chicken Alfredo

Home

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts, sliced thinly
  • 3 cups fettuccine pasta
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cook the fettuccine pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the sliced chicken, season with salt and pepper, and cook until golden and fully cooked. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, saut√© garlic until fragrant (about 1 minute).
  4. Lower the heat and add heavy cream, stirring gently. Gradually mix in Parmesan cheese until the sauce thickens.
  5. Return the chicken to the skillet and toss with the sauce.
  6. Add the cooked fettuccine to the skillet and mix until evenly coated.
  7. Garnish with parsley and serve hot.
