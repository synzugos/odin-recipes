Instructions:

  1. Cook the fettuccine pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the sliced chicken, season with salt and pepper, and cook until golden and fully cooked. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, sauté garlic until fragrant (about 1 minute).
  4. Lower the heat and add heavy cream, stirring gently. Gradually mix in Parmesan cheese until the sauce thickens.
  5. Return the chicken to the skillet and toss with the sauce.
  6. Add the cooked fettuccine to the skillet and mix until evenly coated.
  7. Garnish with parsley and serve hot.
Home Ingredients: 1 lb (450g) beef sirloin, thinly sliced 2 tablespoons soy sauce 2 tablespoons oyster sauce 1 teaspoon cornstarch 2 tablespoons vegetable oil 2 cups mixed vegetables (e.g., bell peppers, broccoli, carrots) 1 clove garlic, minced 1 teaspoon grated ginger 1 tablespoon sesame oil (optional) Instructions: Marinate the beef in soy sauce, oyster sauce, and cornstarch for 15 minutes. Heat vegetable oil in a wok or large skillet over high heat. Stir-fry the beef until browned, then remove and set aside. Add the garlic and ginger to the wok, sautéing briefly. Loaded Baked Potatoes Ingredients: 4 large russet potatoes 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese 1/2 cup cooked bacon bits 1/2 cup sour cream 2 green onions, chopped 2 tablespoons butter Salt and pepper to taste Instructions: Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Wash and dry the potatoes, then poke holes in them with a fork. Rub the potatoes with a little olive oil and sprinkle with salt. Bake directly on the oven rack for about 1 hour, or until tender. Cut a slit in the top of each baked potato and fluff the insides with a fork. Add butter, cheese, bacon bits, and a dollop of sour cream. Sprinkle with green onions. Serve hot and enjoy.
